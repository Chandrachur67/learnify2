### Roles
### Admin
  - Admin can see contents of all courses, lectures and material uploaded by instructors.
  - Admin can approve / reject any instructor / admin signup request. (Manual verification of instructor / admin)
  - Basically Admins have access to everything other than changing course or lecture content of instructors
  - They can Like / comment on videolectures

### Instructor
  - Instructors can create and edit courses through interactive course editing page where thay can see the change in realtime
  - Courses can be paid / free. ( Paid part is yet to be implemented)
  - Instructors can upload free educational videolectures, that is accessible to every logged in user.
  - Instructors can edit their videos through interactive video editing page.
  - They can watch or subscribe other instuctors or courses
  - Instructors can start a live class (using zegoCloud) (working on new UI)
  - They can Like / comment on videolectures

### Users
  - Users can watch videolectures uploaded by Instructors
  - Users can get enrolled in free courses and purchase paid courses and they can access those content
  - Users can join live classes of instructors
  - Users can Like / comment on videolectures


## Exam Corner
Exam Corner is designed to provide all information about competitive exams in one place. Users can find exam dates, useful resources, video lectures, and a link to apply everything together.

## Doubt Corner
An interactive platform enabling students to seek assistance for their queries, including image- based doubts, with instructors and peers providing answers, with instructor responses highlighted.
We are also planning to integrate a AI Chatbot to doubt-corner so that doubts can be address even faster
